HTML Tags Cheat Sheet

Headings

Headings from 'h1 to 'h6' identify blocks and sections. Every page must have one 'h1'.

Paragraphs

Pragraphs of text go in 'p' tags.

Lists

Unordered Lists

Unordered lists are marked with bullet points and go in 'ul' tags. Each list item goes in an 'li' tag.

Types of lists:

  • Unordered lists ('ul')
  • Ordered lists ('ol')

Ordered Lists

Odered lists are numbered and go in 'ol' tags. Each list item goes in 'li' tag.
